# Clayic qualifier (ce), WRB 2022 Ch 5.

A texture class of **clay, sandy clay or silty clay** in one or more
layers with a combined thickness of \>= 30 cm within 100 cm of the
mineral soil surface. Those three classes correspond to clay \>= 40%
(clay / silty clay) or clay \>= 35% with sand \>= 45% (sandy clay) – NOT
the v0.9 proxy of clay \>= 60%, which under-fired (fixed v0.9.130).

## Usage

``` r
qual_clayic(pedon)
```

## Arguments

- pedon:

  A
  [`PedonRecord`](https://hugomachadorodrigues.github.io/soilKey/reference/PedonRecord.md).
