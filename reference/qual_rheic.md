# Rheic qualifier (rh): water-fed Histosol.

WRB 2022 Ch 5 / Ch 4 Histosols (p 96): organic material formed under the
influence of surface or groundwater (the opposite of Ombric).
Distinguished by HIGHER pH and base saturation than Ombric (because the
input water carries dissolved bases). Practical proxy: Histosol Order
with pH_h2o \> 4.5 (above the Ombric ceiling) in the upper 100 cm OR
carbonates / calcium-rich evidence present.

## Usage

``` r
qual_rheic(pedon)
```
