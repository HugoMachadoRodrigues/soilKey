Petrogypsic Subgroup helper – delegate to petrogypsic_horizon_usda

``` r
petrogypsic_subgroup_usda(pedon, max_top_cm = 100)
```

- pedon:

  A
  [`PedonRecord`](https://hugomachadorodrigues.github.io/soilKey/reference/PedonRecord.md).
