# Posic qualifier (po): net positive permanent charge (pH_KCl \> pH_H2O) in some layer at \<= 100 cm. Diagnostic of the most weathered Ferralsols where free Fe / Al oxides dominate the surface charge.

Posic qualifier (po): net positive permanent charge (pH_KCl \> pH_H2O)
in some layer at \<= 100 cm. Diagnostic of the most weathered Ferralsols
where free Fe / Al oxides dominate the surface charge.

## Usage

``` r
qual_posic(pedon)
```

## Arguments

- pedon:

  A
  [`PedonRecord`](https://hugomachadorodrigues.github.io/soilKey/reference/PedonRecord.md).
